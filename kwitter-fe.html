Overview:

Kwitter is a clone of a common micro-blogging platform. I built this during my time at Kenzie Academy on a team of four. I was the scrum master, as well as the lead developer, and was responsible for setting up the meetings, assigning tasks, making sure the team members were getting what needed to be done and most importantly managing our time accordingly. As the lead developer, I was responsible for routing and important features such as: messaging, profile editing, posting.
