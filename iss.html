Overview:

About Myself

I love solving problems!

There is nothing that gives me greater joy than figuring out a better, more optimized and efficient solution to a problem. Software engineering gives me the opportunity to solve all sorts of problems across the entire spectrum almost every single day, and I am grateful for it. One of the things I love about what I do is that coding can be considered as a superpower, because as a software engineer, all you need is an idea, then you can go ahead and not only make your idea a reality, but also make it valuable to the world.

It is this passion that helped me succeed in my year-long education at Kenzie Academy. There I received more knowledge than I could have ever imagined to fuel my hunger for learning. Starting with project-based, hands-on experience with the back-end, as well as the front-end of web development, while diving deep into data structures and algorithms to building APIs, databases and responsive web applications from ground up. I also learned a lot about the true importance of adaptability, teamwork, communication, project management and most importantly, time management when it comes to delivering the best possible product. Last but not least, it was here where I discovered how much there is yet to be learned and why you must never stop learning.
